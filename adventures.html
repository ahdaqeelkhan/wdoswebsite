Have some fun!

We offer various tours, just for you!

Crystal Haven Hotel is the people's hotel. You're happiness and satisfaction is our happiness. That is why we offer three fun-filled activities that will help you enjoy your stay even more!

Cycling

Embark on a thrilling Desert Safari at Crystal Haven Hotel and delve into the enchanting beauty of Dubai's desert landscapes. Our safari adventure promises an unforgettable journey into the heart of the desert, where you'll encounter majestic sand dunes, immerse yourself in the rich culture of the region, and experience the thrill of traditional desert activities such as dune bashing, camel riding, and sandboarding. As the sun sets over the desert horizon, savor a delectable Arabian feast under the starlit skies at our desert camp. Our expert guides and drivers ensure your safety and provide an informative and entertaining experience. Whether you seek adventure, cultural exploration, or simply a serene escape, our Desert Safari offers a captivating and immersive way to discover the magic of Dubai's desert landscapes.

Watersports

Indulge in the exhilarating Watersports Adventure at Crystal Haven Hotel, where aquatic excitement meets Dubai's stunning coastline. Whether you're an experienced water enthusiast or a curious beginner, our range of watersports activities has something for everyone. Zoom across the waves on a jet ski, find tranquility while paddleboarding, or explore hidden coves through kayaking. Our certified instructors ensure your safety and provide expert guidance, while top-quality equipment guarantees an enjoyable experience. Choose from flexible packages tailored to your preferences, whether you prefer a thrilling solo adventure or a group excursion. Amidst Dubai's iconic skyline and breathtaking coastal vistas, our Watersports Adventure promises unforgettable moments and a unique way to elevate your stay at Crystal Haven Hotel. Dive in and embrace the thrill of Dubai's waters!
